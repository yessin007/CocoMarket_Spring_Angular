Main Features

With the advanced features of BEE Pro you can organize email projects by brand, restrict user access and manage brand-specific settings. 

 

Support for multiple brands

and sending platforms.
 

Assign Projects to Brands to better manage your email creation workflow. And separately connect each brand/client to their favorite sending tool, such as MailChimp, HubSpot, SendGrid etc.

Ask for feedback.
Get to approval. Fast.
 

Are you happy with your campaign?

Easily invite clients or colleagues to review an email and pin comments to it, visually. Get to approval faster, with no messy email threads.
 

Add team members. Control what they can do.
 

You can easily grant and remove permissions to access a brand. And you can leverage user roles to decide who can do what in each brand, such as restricting access to locked content.

Choose specific settings

for each brand
 

For each brand you can define custom templates, add custom fonts, users and connectors to sending platforms. You can also insert merge tags or special links, compatible with your sending software.
